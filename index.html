The Fine Print

Club Charter

The Premise

Why a spreadsheet exists for burgers.

SBCS is a small, serious-but-not-that-serious supper club in Shanghai. We meet, we order the burger, and we fill in the card. The point isn't to be right — it's to be consistent, so that "an 8" means the same thing in Jing'an as it does in Xuhui.

The Rules

Short enough to remember mid-bite.

  • One card per person per burger. Score before you discuss.
  • Totals are recomputed from the 20 sub-scores. The sum is the truth.
  • Every score is public. The ledger is the club.
  • No plate too big or small. Smash, pub, wagyu — all welcome.
